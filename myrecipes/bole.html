Bole and Fish

Description

Bole and Fish is cherished delicacy eaten by Port Harcourt people of Rivers State, Nigeria. It consists of roasted plantain, popularly called Bole and roasted peppered fish, eaten with pepper sauce and vegetable to taste.

Ingredients

  • Plantain
  • Fish
  • Palm Oil
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Vegetable
